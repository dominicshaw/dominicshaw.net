Pattern Analysis & Applications (PAA) Journal Jun 2004 - Nov 2004

I was brought in by my 3rd year tutor after the successful completion of my dissertation. He ran PAA, a journal operating out of Exeter University, and hired me to write a paper submission system in PHP / MySql. The dissertation had been similar but in ASP.NET and SQL and he wanted to run it off a Linux box before Mono was established. This was a fast introduction to commercial software development and a steep but enjoyable learning curve.
